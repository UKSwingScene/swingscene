About SwingScene UK

SwingScene UK is the most comprehensive listing of swingers club events in the United Kingdom. We aggregate upcoming special events and themed nights from 27 clubs across England, updated every day automatically.

Whether you're looking for a one-off themed party, a big anniversary night, or a fetish event at your favourite club, SwingScene gives you a single place to see what's coming up — without having to check 27 different websites.

How It Works

Every morning at 2am, our system visits each club's website and pulls their upcoming events. The results are filtered to show only named special events.

A small number of clubs whose websites can't be read automatically are updated manually, so nothing gets missed.
